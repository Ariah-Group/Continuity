This screen is available only to Local Administrators.
  • Use the "Access" selection to "turn on" or "turn off" the UC Ready tool for ALL of your users (except Local Administrators). You will want to turn it "off" to your users anytime you are using these "Administrator Control Screens" to alter the configuration of the tool.
  • The 4 check-boxes under "Screen Options" determine the display for ALL users at your location (campus, med center, etc.). The first 3 boxes show-or-hide entire screens. The 4th will show-or-hide the "See Examples from Other Departments" button on the "Step 2: Critical Functions" screen. These choices are reversible - change them at any time.
  • The optional Skills screen collects information on skills that may be needed by the department in times of crisis. Consideration is being given to including a skills available screen in the next version of the UC Ready tool.
