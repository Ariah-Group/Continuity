List of Instructional Departments:

In the grid below you will populate the drop-down list that appears on the “Identify Your Department” screen in Step 4: Instruction.

The purpose of this list is to provide the names of all departments on campus that offer instruction. The best and easiest place to obtain these department names is usually the course catalog (you might also obtain them from a person who has access to the database of courses).

The reason for providing this list to the user is to ensure that the department names are the official names, and align perfectly with the official list of courses.

Department names will sort alphabetically.
